About CitizenComplaint

CitizenComplaint is an innovative platform that empowers citizens to easily file complaints and applications to government officials. With user-friendly templates, email submission, and real-time tracking, we aim to simplify and digitize the complaint process, ensuring transparency and accountability in governance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

How can I track my complaint status?

Log into your account and navigate to the "My Complaints" section. You'll find all your submitted complaints and their current status.
