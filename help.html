Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Visit the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the email instructions to reset it securely.

How can I track my complaint?

Go to your dashboard and check the "My Complaints" section. You'll see real-time updates there.

What are Premium features?

Premium users get faster processing, AI-generated drafts, and access to expert support via chat and email.

Can I file a complaint without Aadhaar?

Yes, you can file a complaint using basic details, but Aadhaar verification unlocks faster routing and premium benefits.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and handled according to India's Digital Personal Data Protection Bill, 2023.
